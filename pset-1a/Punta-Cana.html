[ Punta Cana ]

in the Dominican Republic

Basic Information

Punta Cana is a beautiful town on the easternmost tip of the Dominican Republic. This place is a very popular vacation destination because of its clear waters and beautiful beaches. There are many resorts in Punta Cana and many activities. The most popular activities are zip-lining, windsurfing, kayaking and sailing. Some of the most popular resorts in Punta Cana are Sanctuary Cap Cana, Majestic Colonial Punta Cana, Punta Cana Princess, etc.
